Privacy Policy - Task Reminder

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Data Collection

Task Reminder does not collect, transmit, or share personal data. Tasks and settings are stored only on your device.

Data Export

You can manually export your tasks as a JSON file. Export is initiated by you and saved to a location you choose. No export data is sent to external servers by the app itself.

Third-Party Services

The app does not include analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services.
