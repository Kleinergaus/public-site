Sustainable Apps by Tobias

I am an independent app developer building practical tools that respect users. No pay-to-win loops, no addictive mechanics, and no unnecessary data transfer.

My goal is simple: create useful apps that respect your time, your privacy, and the planet by avoiding wasteful network activity whenever possible.

Core Principles

Respectful by Design

No manipulative monetization and no pay-to-win mechanics.

Data Minimalism

Only transfer data when truly needed, with local-first app behavior.

Useful, Not Noisy

Apps should solve real problems without bloat, ads, or dark patterns.
