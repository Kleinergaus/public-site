Privacy Policy – Task Reminder

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Data collection

Task Reminder does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data (tasks and settings) is stored exclusively on your device.

Data export

The app allows you to manually export your tasks as a JSON file. This action is always initiated by you and the file is saved to a location you choose. No data is sent to any server.

Third-party services

The app does not use any analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services.
