MathMerge

Planned educational game - concept phase.

Idea

MathMerge is planned as an educational merge game. You play a classic merge-style loop, and instead of watching ads to continue or unlock benefits, you solve short math tasks.

Learning Focus

The first focus is helping kids learn and practice multiplication tables in a playful and motivating way.

How Progress Works

Progress and rewards are tied to learning: solving tasks unlocks the next steps. This keeps the game fair, useful, and free from ad-based pressure.

Principles for This App

  • No pay-to-win progression.
  • No ad-gating that forces users to watch ads to continue.
  • Learning-first progression with meaningful math practice.
  • Minimal data transfer and local-first logic where possible.

Status
